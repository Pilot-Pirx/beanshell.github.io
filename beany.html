Beany the JavaBean


Who is Beany and what is he all about you ask?

  • Beany is the BeanShell mascot. He is a JavaBean.
  • Beany is in a constant state of surprise and amazement at the features of BeanShell; hence his gaping maw.
  • Beany never lies. He always tells the truth about bugs.
  • Beany is lovable. By all means, feel free to love him.

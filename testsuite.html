BeanShell Test Suite and TCK

This section will hold information on the BeanShell Test Suite and Technology Compatability Kit (TCK).

A minimal language test suite is included with the BeanShell source distribution. It consists of about 120 test files covering most aspects of the language in basic ways.

The development verison uses a combination of junit and bsh test scripts.
